Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026  ·  App: Short Video Auto Uploader

1. Overview

Short Video Auto Uploader ("the App") is a personal automation tool used solely by its developer to upload video content to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in connection with the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from third parties. The only data processed is:

  • OAuth access tokens for TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram — stored locally on the developer's own device and never shared externally.
  • Video files and metadata (title, description, tags) — provided by the developer and uploaded directly to the respective platforms via their official APIs.

3. How Information Is Used

Access tokens and video data are used solely for the purpose of authenticating with and uploading content to the connected platform APIs. No data is used for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

4. Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with any third party. Video content is transmitted only to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram via their official APIs, as directed by the developer.

5. Data Storage

All credentials and tokens are stored locally on the developer's own machine. No cloud database or external server is used to store user data.

6. Third-Party Platforms

This App interacts with the following platforms via their official APIs. Their respective privacy policies apply to data processed on their end:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Meta / Instagram Privacy Policy

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
